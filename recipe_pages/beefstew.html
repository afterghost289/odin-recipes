Beef Stew

Ingredients

  1. Beef stew meat
  2. Beef
  3. Beef bone broth or chicken bone broth, or just regular broths as well
  4. Vegetables, you can get creative with it and choose your own and the amount you prefer
  • Carrots
  • Onions
  • Potatoes
  • Beets
  • Sweet potatoes
  • Bok Choy

Herbs/Spices (you can experiment)

  • Bay Leaves
  • Black pepper
  • Coriander seeds
  • Salt
  • Rosemary
  • Sage
  • Paprika
  • Some olive oil

Directions

  1. Cut up Vegetables, diced or chopped
  2. Toss chopped or diced vegetables into crockpot, portions of each vegetable are up to you, fill it to halfway or more if you'd like. (leave space for beef)
  3. Add olive oil to your pot of vegetables, add spices and the portions you want for flavoring (I prefer to experiment with flavors and spices)
  4. Hand mix and toss around until vegetables are all covered in spices and oil
  5. Spice the beef (salt, pepper, can add other spices you'd like) and sear for about 6 minutes not thoroughly cooked
  6. After the beef is seared add it to the pot and mix it in with the vegetables
  7. Next add the beef bone broth or chicken bone/regular broth to the pot and fill it over whats in there if you need more add water to it
  8. Now put your stew on the crock pot, for 250-320 degrees, on slow cook for 8 hours
  9. Check stew if you want around 6 or 7 hours and mix it or even add some more things if it needs more flavoring

Scrambled Eggs with Avocado Toast

Ingredients

  1. 2-3 eggs
  2. Bread
  3. 1-2 avocados
  4. Spinach
  5. Grape/cherry tomatoes

Herbs/Spices

  • Salt
  • Pepper
  • Paprika if you want some spice

Directions

  1. Cut and prepare avocados and take out pit.
  2. Halve tomatoes.
  3. Wash spinach if needed.
  4. Turn on heat to medium/high for stovetop, drizzle some oil onto a frying pan.
  5. Crack 2-3 eggs into frying pan.
  6. Cook eggs for about 8-15m until done and scramble for a bit, add spices as well.
  7. add bread into toaster as it cooks.
  8. when toast is done, spread avocado onto it.
  9. Finally add eggs on top of toast and add spinach and tomatoes to the top of it.

Image of mediterranean Salmon

Mediterranean Salmon

Ingredients

  • 1 tsp dried oregano
  • 1 sp garlic powder
  • 1 tsp basil
  • 1/2 tsp rosemary
  • 1/2 tsp smoked or sweet paprika
  • 1/2 tsp sea salt
  • 1/4 tsp black pepper
  • 1 lb salmon fillets

Directions

  1. Use spices as a dry rub and rub onto salmon fillets.
  2. Preheat oven to 400 degrees.
  3. Place skin down in a pan with aluminum foil.
  4. Bake the salmon for 12-15 mintues in the oven. Until the salmon flakes easily with a fork.
  5. Garnish with lemon wedges or tomatoes, cucumbers, olives, feta for more mediterranean style.
